abstract: This roundtable explores the relationship between economic activity, human behaviour, and transport demand, with a focus on reimagining forecasting, prediction, and modelling approaches. It examines how to move beyond traditional data sources by incorporating real-time data and the concept of "human weather" using population movement as a predictive tool. The discussion addresses challenges such as accounting for diverse and unpredictable behaviours, developing more localised and adaptive models, governance and guidance, and better alignment between regional and national planning priorities.
